Tteokbokki Recipe

Description

Tteokbokki is a street food that originates from Korea. It consists of rice cakes, egg, radish, fish cakes, and a blend of vegetables. It usually comes spicy but there are non-spicy options.

Ingredients

  • Korean rice cakes
  • Anchovy stock packet
  • Hard boiled egg
  • Gochujang(red pepper paste)
  • Korean red pepper powder
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the gochujang together with the red pepper powder, soy sauce, garlic, and sugar until it becomes pasty
  2. Mix the anchovy stock with water until it turns soupy
  3. Add paste to soup and mix
  4. Add rice cakes and any other ingredients not already in
  5. Boil for about 5 to 7 minutes
