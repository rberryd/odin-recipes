Acai Bowl Recipe

Description

Acai Bowl is a healthy smoothie-like food with toppings such as bananas and strawberries. They originate from Brazil and are very popular in big cities.

Ingredients

  • Pure Unsweetened Acai Puree
  • Frozen blueberries and strawberries
  • Banana
  • Liquid(preferably milk)
  • Yogurt
  • Granola
  • Seeds
  • Nuts
  • Fruits

Instructions

  1. Add the puree, frozen fruits, and liquid of your choice in a bowl
  2. Blend the combined ingredients together
  3. Add toppings
  4. You're all good to go!
